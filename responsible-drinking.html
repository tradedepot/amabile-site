Please enjoy responsibly

Good times, in good measure

We take friendship seriously — and that means looking out for each other, too.

Amabile di Rosa is for adults of legal drinking age — 18+, or the legal drinking age in your country. We love wine as part of good times with good people, always enjoyed responsibly.

Please don't drink and drive, and don't drink if you're pregnant or under the legal drinking age. Pace yourself, look after your friends, and keep water close.

If you or someone you know needs support around alcohol, please reach out to a local health service or helpline — there's no shame in asking for help.
